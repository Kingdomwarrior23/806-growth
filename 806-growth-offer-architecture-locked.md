## 4. The GBP Posting Cap (Critical Scope Protection)

**Growth Tier:** "2–4 template-based Google Business Profile posts per month"  
**Overlay Pro:** "2 template-based Google Business Profile posts per month"  
**Overlay Lite:** No monthly GBP posting (one-time setup/cleanup only)

**Internal Fulfillment Rules:**
- Templates only: events, offers, updates, seasonal
- No custom photography shoots included
- No more than the stated monthly count
- If client wants more → upsell GBP Post Pack ($147 for 4 more/mo, $247 for 8)
- If client wants custom photography → upsell Monthly Content Day ($297/mo)

**Why This Matters:** Keeps Growth packed as the hero offer while preventing "can you write a custom post every day?" scope creep.

## 7. Sales Framework

### Anchor High First
Lead with AI Revenue → Growth (most popular) → Smart-Site/Overlay.

### Discovery Questions (first 60 seconds)
1. "Do you already have a website you're happy with?" → Routes Build vs Overlay
2. "What happens when someone calls you after hours right now?" → Sells MCTB
3. "How are you getting Google reviews today?" → Sells review automation

## 8. Onboarding & Offboarding SOP Pointers

### Day 0–7 Onboarding Checklist (Solo Operator)
- Welcome email + kickoff call scheduled
- Client intake form (business info, branding, photos, services, target customer)
- Access collection: domain registrar, DNS, current site login, GBP manager access, Facebook/Instagram admin, Google Workspace (if needed)
- GHL sub-account provisioned from niche snapshot
- Kickoff call: review scope, set expectations, confirm launch date (within 14 days)
- Internal Asana/Notion task created with all fulfillment steps

### Offboarding / Cancel Policy (Honors "You Own It" Guarantee)
- Export all contacts, conversations, and data to CSV + deliver to client
- Transfer domain to client's registrar
- Remove 806 Growth branding from GBP/site
- Deliver final report + access credentials
- 30-day wind-down period; no punitive cancel fees
- Offer re-engagement path (come back anytime, snapshot preserved)
